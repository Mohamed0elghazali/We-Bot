* All bundles prices are tax exclusive
  * WE Gold line price is 81.23 EGP for all price points
  * The monthly invoice will be issued on either 1st or the 16th of each month according to the customer’s bill cycle
  * Customers are not eligible to downgrade WE Gold bundle before 6 months and up to 12 months in case of receiving special dials and based on the price point
  * WE Gold customer is responsible for paying all contracting fees, change of ownership and service installation fees for Fixed Internet, WE Air and Landline.
  * 140 El-Dalil minutes are granted to WE GOLD customers on a monthly basis, once consumed any additional usage will be charged at the standard rate of EGP 1.50 per minute.
